Challenges

You will receive the flag{} when you win the 3 challenges at one time.

Your challenges will be re-generated when you submit a WRONG solution.

Your solution must be like: f1,f2,f3,...,fn where fi has appeared in the index, e.g. "cos,exp,log,tanh" will leave 0.7615941559557649 (≈tanh(log(exp(cos(0))))) on the screen.

You can use python-requests to implement the magic! see: poc.py
